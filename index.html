About Deltron – Leading Fire Protection Company in Qatar

Deltron Trading & Services is a trusted fire protection company in Qatar, offering a wide range of safety and MEP solutions. Formerly known as Fire Faster Trading for fire and safety equipment, we have grown into a diversified business with strong values and a focus on quality. Founded in 2020 by a team of dedicated professionals, Deltron is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company and is officially listed with the Qatar Civil Defense Department.
