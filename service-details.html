Fire Alarm Systems in Qatar

Reliable Fire Alarm Company in Qatar

Deltron is a leading fire alarm company in Qatar, providing comprehensive fire detection systems that meet local safety regulations and international standards. Our systems are designed to detect fires at the earliest stage and trigger quick alerts, ensuring life safety and property protection.

Our Fire Alarm Services Include:

  • System Design & Planning – Customized solutions based on the layout and usage of your facility.
  • Supply & Installation – QCD-approved fire alarm panels, detectors, and accessories.
  • Testing & Commissioning – Ensuring system reliability and regulatory compliance.
  • Maintenance & Support – Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) and on-call support for 24/7 protection.
  • System Upgrades & Integration – Retrofitting older systems or integrating with building management systems.
  • QCD Approval Assistance – Full coordination with Qatar Civil Defence for certifications and inspections.

Types of Fire Alarm Systems We Offer

  • Conventional Fire Alarm Systems – Cost-effective and ideal for small buildings or simple layouts.
  • Addressable Fire Alarm Systems – Intelligent systems that identify the exact source of alarms or faults.
  • Wireless Fire Alarm Systems – Flexible solutions for spaces where cabling is limited or restricted.
  • Voice Evacuation Systems – Automated voice alerts for safe and orderly evacuation during emergencies.
