Fire Pumps and Fire Hose Reels

We offer complete installation of wet and dry riser fire hydrant systems, hose reel systems and sprinkler systems. Fire hydrant/hose reel systems as an installation of pipes, water tanks, pumps, hydrant outlets and/or hose reels in a building to provide a ready means by which a jet of water can be delivered to any part of the building for the purpose of firefighting. We provide a comprehensive range of related services including design, supply, installation, testing and maintenance

  • Fire pumps provide water for firefighting when municipal water systems are not sufficient.
  • Fire hose reels are installed to provide a quick source of water for initial fire suppression.
  • Regular maintenance and testing are crucial for the reliability of fire pumps and fire hose reels.

Fire pumps and fire hose reels are important components of a fire protection system, which is designed to prevent and control fires in buildings or other structures.

Both fire pumps and fire hose reels require regular maintenance and testing to ensure they are in good working order and can be relied upon in the event of a fire emergency.
