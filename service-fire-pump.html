Fire Pumps and Fire Hose Reels

We offer complete installation of wet and dry riser fire hydrant systems, hose reel systems and sprinkler systems. Fire hydrant/hose reel systems as an installation of pipes, water tanks, pumps, hydrant outlets and/or hose reels in a building to provide a ready means by which a jet of water can be delivered to any part of the building for the purpose of firefighting. We provide a comprehensive range of related services including design, supply, installation, testing and maintenance
